How to find us

1. Find room 114 on the first floor of Herter Hall. To the left there are downstairs.

2. Go downstairs. Then turn right.

3. Pass the restrooms. There are small stairs at the end of the hallway.

4. Go down and turn right.

5. Walk straight. There is a translation center area with tables to the left, come inside.

6. Right before the second entrance there is a door to the right, this is the room you’re looking for.

Hurray, you’ve found us! Welcome in!🥳
